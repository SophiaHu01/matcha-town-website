Our Ingredients

A global synthesis of the finest sources

Japanese Stone-Ground Matcha

Our matcha comes from Japan's finest tea regions, where centuries of tradition meet modern sustainability practices.

Stone-ground to preserve delicate flavor compounds and vibrant color, each batch is carefully selected for its umami depth, natural sweetness, and ceremonial-grade quality. This is the heart of everything we create.

French Artisan Ingredients

We source select ingredients from France's artisan producers, bringing Old World craft to New World innovation.

From delicate floral extracts to premium dairy, these ingredients add layers of complexity and refinement to our creations, honoring the French tradition of pâtisserie excellence.

Italian Gelato Craft

Made fresh daily using traditional Italian gelato techniques.

Lower fat content and slower churning create our signature smooth, creamy texture while allowing the pure matcha flavor to shine. Every scoop is a balance of tradition and innovation.
